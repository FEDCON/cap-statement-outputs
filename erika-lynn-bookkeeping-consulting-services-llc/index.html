01Company Overview

Erika Lynn Bookkeeping and Consulting Services, LLC is a woman-owned small business delivering professional bookkeeping, financial recordkeeping, and accounting consulting services to small businesses, nonprofits, and government contractors. Founded and led by Erika Rebout, the firm provides accurate, audit-ready financial records and compliance-focused reporting that supports sound decision-making and regulatory accountability. Operating remotely with nationwide reach, the firm serves clients who require reliable, timely, and organized financial management without the overhead of a full-time accounting department.
