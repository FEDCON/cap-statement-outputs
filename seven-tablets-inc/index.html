01Company Overview

Seven Tablets Inc is a nationwide AI and software development firm founded in 2013, delivering custom software, digital transformation, and IT staffing solutions to commercial and government clients across the United States. The firm operates under a Business First, Technology Follows philosophy, aligning stakeholders on outcomes before writing a single line of code, a model that has consistently produced measurable ROI for clients including Sirius XM, Regions Bank, and TD Bank. With 30 professionals, a proprietary AI development platform, and partnerships with AWS, IBM Watson, and Salesforce, Seven Tablets brings enterprise-grade capability to federal engagements ranging from $40,000 to $2.5 million.
