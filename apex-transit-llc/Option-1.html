Company Overview

Apex Transit, LLC delivers end-to-end freight transportation and logistics coordination across commercial and government supply chains. Since 2012, we have provided FTL/LTL, expedited shipping, final-mile delivery, and comprehensive supply chain management services. Our technology-enabled solutions include route optimization, multi-modal coordination, real-time tracking, and warehouse operations. We consistently achieve cost reductions up to 15%, improve on-time performance to 98%, and maintain 100% shipment visibility through advanced TMS and GPS tracking systems.

Past Performance
  • Nyco Chemical: Assumed full 3PL operations within one week after prior provider termination. Managed LTL, FTL, drayage, and international shipments with two-person team. Achieved 15% freight cost reduction, improved on-time delivery from 93% to 98%, and reduced expedited shipping reliance by 27% through optimized routing and carrier selection. Ongoing relationship since initial one-year engagement.
  • Discount Tire: Managed complete warehouse relocation including 15 truckloads daily and 60 days secure trailer storage. Led by one account manager and five drivers, completed project 30 days ahead of schedule in 90 days versus planned four months. Reduced storage costs 10% through accelerated completion with zero security incidents or losses throughout high-volume operation.
