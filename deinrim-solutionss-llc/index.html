01Company Overview

Deinrim Solutions LLC is a Georgia-based IT managed services and modernization firm delivering enterprise-grade cloud migration, cybersecurity, service desk operations, and AI integration to commercial and government clients. Founded in 2021, the firm is led by a founder with over two decades of managed IT experience supporting Fortune 50 organizations, and maintains strategic partnerships with major cloud service providers and hardware manufacturers. Deinrim supports environments ranging from $25K task orders to $2M programs, serving small agencies and large multi-site organizations with equal capability.
