01Company Overview

BioBond Adhesives, Inc. develops and manufactures a full line of plant-based adhesives and protective coatings engineered for high-performance, low-VOC, and zero-added-microplastic applications across federal facilities, defense environments, healthcare, food processing, and construction. Product lines include BioCoat Protective Coatings, BioAdhere Adhesives, BioMelt Hot Melt, and BioStart Intermediates, all produced domestically and formulated without harsh odors or compromise on performance. BioBond serves government and commercial clients nationwide, delivering cost-effective, compliant coating solutions suitable for the most demanding regulated environments, from assisted living and veterinary facilities to mobility and defense applications.

02Past Performance & Key Projects
Defense and Mobility Environments: Supplied plant-based protective coatings formulated for defense and mobility applications, delivering abrasion, corrosion, and UV resistance in demanding operational settings across multiple military-adjacent facilities, achieving full compliance with applicable MIL-spec surface protection standards.
Food Processing and Distribution Facilities: Provided low-VOC, food-safe BioCoat floor and surface coatings across food processing and distribution facilities, meeting FDA and USDA hygiene standards and supporting continuous operations in occupied production environments.
Healthcare and Assisted Living Facilities: Delivered mold-, bacteria-, and virus-resistant coating systems to veterinary clinics, assisted living centers, and public restroom facilities, achieving infection-control compliance benchmarks required by state health and CMS regulatory frameworks.
Commercial Construction and Garage Applications: Applied two-component polyurethane and Carbyne coating systems to concrete, ceramic, and metal substrates across commercial construction and garage projects nationwide, with documented service life exceeding standard polyaspartic alternatives and zero reported rework incidents.
03Core Competencies
USA Domestic Manufacturing: all product lines are formulated and produced domestically, ensuring Buy American compliance.
Plant-Based Adhesive Formulation: bio-derived adhesives bond reliably to concrete, wood, ceramic, and metal substrates.
Protective Coating Development: BioCoat systems deliver abrasion, UV, corrosion, and microbial resistance for federal use.
Low-VOC Water-Based Systems: zero-added-microplastic, low-odor coatings meet standards for regulated and occupied environments.
Hot Melt and Intermediate Chemistry: BioMelt and BioStart products serve packaging, textile, and industrial applications.
Two-Component Polyurethane Coatings: Carbyne system provides twice the pot life of polyaspartic coatings, reducing rework.
04Differentiators
  • 100% USA Manufactured: domestic production supports Buy American Act compliance and eliminates foreign supply chain risk
  • Zero Added Microplastics: all formulations contain no added microplastics, meeting emerging federal environmental standards
  • 2x Longer Pot Life: Carbyne two-component system outperforms polyaspartic coatings, reducing application errors and rework costs
  • Food-Safe and Healthcare-Compliant: coatings validated for food processing, veterinary, and assisted living environments requiring strict hygiene standards
  • Broad Substrate Compatibility: single product family bonds and coats concrete, ceramic, wood, and metal across diverse federal facility types
