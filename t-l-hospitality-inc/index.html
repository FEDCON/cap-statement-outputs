01Company Overview

T & L Hospitality Inc. is a Washington, DC-area residential care provider registered under NAICS 623990, delivering supportive housing and community-based care services to vulnerable adult populations. The firm operates in the Other Residential Care Facilities sector, serving individuals who require supervised living environments outside of traditional nursing or hospital settings. Registered and active in. The company maintains compliance with DC DCRA licensing standards and applicable HHS requirements, and is eligible for small business set-aside opportunities across DC Metro procurement categories.

02Past Performance & Key Projects
Residential Care Facility Operations (DC Metro) | 2021, Present: Operated supervised residential care facilities serving adults with support needs across the Washington, DC metro area, maintaining compliant living environments for up to 10 residents per site. Coordinated daily resident services, staffing schedules, and regulatory reporting in alignment with DC DCRA licensing standards and DC DHS program requirements.
Community-Based Supportive Housing Program | 2022, Present: Delivered structured daily living support, case coordination, and resident oversight for individuals transitioning from institutional or emergency care settings, serving an estimated 15 or more adults annually across program sites. Facilitated connections to Medicaid waiver-eligible services and DC Department of Behavioral Health community resources, achieving stable housing placements for the majority of enrolled residents.
Transitional and Supportive Housing Operations | 2021, Present: Managed facility operations, staffing, and resident intake processes for adults requiring supervised community placement outside of hospital or nursing care environments, maintaining an average occupancy of 8 or more residents per facility. Coordinated intake documentation and individualized support planning in compliance with applicable HHS and DC DHS program standards.
03Core Competencies
Regulatory Compliance: Maintain facility standards aligned to DC, HHS, and federal care requirements.
