01Company Overview

Aegis Industrial Supply is an SDVOSB/VOSB-certified MRO supplier and facilities support firm serving federal and state government clients across the Southeast and nationwide. Founded by a 34-year U.S. Army veteran and a supply chain professional with deep industrial and fabrication management experience, the firm delivers industrial supplies, hardware, facility maintenance, and machining services through partnerships with ISO 9001 certified manufacturers, MSC Industrial, and Airgas. Aegis holds a $1M bonding capacity and a perfect on-time delivery record across all completed government engagements, with active certifications supporting set-aside eligibility at agencies ranging from the Defense Logistics Agency to state departments of health and transportation. The company operates across Alabama, Georgia, Florida, Tennessee, Michigan, and South Carolina, with nationwide delivery capability supporting clients wherever mission requirements arise.

02Past Performance & Key Projects
State of Alabama, Department of Transportation: Fulfilled an MRO supply order replacing propane tanks at the Guntersville site with updated, code-compliant units on a $2,000 contract. Aegis sourced the correct tank specifications, coordinated delivery directly to the site, and completed the engagement with 100% on-time delivery and zero deficiencies noted by the contracting officer.
State of Alabama, Department of Health: Upgraded facility lighting from fluorescent to LED across state health resources buildings in Alabama, delivering improved illumination, reduced maintenance burden, and measurable energy savings on a $4,800 contract completed on schedule. Aegis managed product sourcing, site coordination, and installation support, ensuring all fixtures met applicable energy efficiency and safety standards for occupied government facilities.
